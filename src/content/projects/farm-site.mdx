## Overview

**Pine Ridge Beef** is a polished Astro demo meant for local farms selling **¼, ½, and whole beef shares**.  
The focus is clarity: plain-language pricing, USDA processing notes, and a CTA that stays obvious on mobile.

Highlights:

- **Transparent pricing cards** with estimated totals & freezer space
- **How-it-works timeline** from reservation to pickup/delivery
- **Gallery** that sets expectations (pasture → processor → packaged beef)
- **Reserve form** (no payment) to capture intent with the right details
- **CLS-safe images** using fixed dimensions and responsive sources
